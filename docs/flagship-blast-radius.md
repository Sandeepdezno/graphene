## Blast-radius traversal rule

Blast radius of a node **X** = X's forward impact footprint. Starting from X,
follow, transitively (shortest hop wins):

- from a **Program/FunctionModule**: its outbound `WRITES` (direct *and* inferred),
  outbound `CALLS`, and (Programs only) outbound `IMPLEMENTS`;
- from a **Program**: also the **Jobs that `EXECUTE` it** (they fail if it breaks);
- from a **Table**: the **Programs/FMs that `READ` it** (they consume X's output);
- **Jobs** and **BAdIs** are terminal.

Outbound `READS` (a node's *inputs*) are **not** traversed — an input is not
downstream of X. This is why `ZTABLE_EXCHANGE_RATES` / `ZTABLE_TAX_RATES` (inputs
to the called FMs) are in the graph but **not** in the blast radius.
